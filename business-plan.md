Shop El Gato — Business Plan (summary)

Executive summary:
Shop El Gato makes handcrafted team items (bracelets, custom bags, keychains) with free design mockups. Local delivery only, cash-on-delivery. URL: shopelgato

Target customers: local parents, students, booster clubs, sports teams in Los Gatos, CA.

Products & pricing examples:
- Team bracelet: $6–$12
- Custom bag: $18–$35
- Keychain: $4–$10
- Bulk discounts for 20+ items

Services:
- Free design mockups
- Local delivery, cash on delivery
- Orders via email/DM/phone

Operations & marketing:
- Handmade in-house, small batches
- Instagram + local partnerships; flyers at school events

3-month goals:
- Launch site and social, 50 orders/month target, partner with 1 team

Risks:
- Cash handling: keep receipts, limited change; require order confirmation.
